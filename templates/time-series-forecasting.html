How to Use in Your ETL Pipeline
  1. Data Preparation: Ensure your time series data is clean and properly formatted
  2. Model Training: Use historical data to train forecasting models
  3. Automated Forecasting: Schedule regular forecast generation in your ETL jobs
  4. Integration: Store forecasts in your data warehouse for business intelligence
  5. Monitoring: Track forecast accuracy and retrain models as needed
  6. Alerting: Set up notifications for significant forecast deviations
